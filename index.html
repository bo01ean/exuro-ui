What is Burner Podcast?

It's a show for Burners and the Burner-curious. It's a casual conversation with interesting Burners (that's redundant) who are doing amazing things in the community as well as in the Default World. Burner Podcast began is a direct interview show but it has since developed in content to feature camp profiles, shows about how Burning Man works and roundtables on topics that affect the Burner community. Episodes generally feel like you are dropping into a conversation with friends which is great for those gloomy Post Burn Blues.
